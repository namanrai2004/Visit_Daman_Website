Water-Park

Mirasol-Lake-Garden

Nov 12

Looking for peace during your trip to Daman? Then Mirasol Lake Garden is the places to go. Blanketed by a green carpet, and numerous trees, this garden is one of the best places to visit in Daman to indulge in an unending affair with nature. The calmness and serenity of the place provide you an opportunity to spend quality time with your loved ones and engage in fun-filled activities for the purpose of entertainment. Location: Near Kadaiya Village, Nani Daman Timings: 10 am to 6 pm Entry Fee: INR 10 for adults & INR 5 for children Things To Do: Boating, and enjoying fun filled rides in the amusement area of the park Time Required: 1 hour. How To Reach: Bus to Mirosal water park. What’s Special: Boat rides, beautiful lake. Entry Fee: INR 30, kids – free

Fort

Moti-Daman-Fort

Nov 11

Constructed by the Portuguese, towards the end of sixteenth century, the Moti Daman Fort is one of the most popular monuments in Daman. Even though the fort has worn out, and needs restoration, it is still one of the best places to visit in Daman which boasts of Colonial style architecture featuring huge walls, 10 bastions, 2 gateways, and an Iberian church. Location: Moti Daman, Daman and Diu Timings: 8 am to 4 pm Entry Fee: INR 10 to INR 15 per person

Soak In The View

Light-House

Nov 12

Wondering what to see in Daman? Then plan a visit to the colossal Lighthouse in Daman. Located in the vicinity of the Moti Daman Fort, Lighthouse is one of the most popular Daman tourist attractions. A large number of tourists flock to this place to spectate the marvelous sight of sunset, witness the panoramic views of the Arabian Sea, and the city. It is also a perfect place for photography lovers. Location: Rua Martim Afonso, Fort Area, Moti Daman, Daman, Daman Time Required: 1-2 hours How To Reach: Take a cab from Modi Daman What’s Special: Taking pictures during sunrise, long walls Entry Fee: Free

Cathedral Of Bom Jesus

Nov 11

Due to a strong connection with Portuguese, Daman is populated with Catholics which makes Cathedral of Bom Jesus one of the top Daman tourist places. The construction of this cathedral can be dated back to 1603 and still the building stands strong. The beautiful altarpiece along with the intricately carved doorway has attracted several tourists till now. One must visit this tourist place in Daman to witness the craftsmanship and architecture of this place. Location: Manguerial Rd, Fort Area, Moti Daman, Daman, Daman and Diu 396220 Timings: 7 am – 9 pm Time Required: 1 hour. How To Reach: 10kms from Panjim tax stand, local cabs available. Entry Fee: Free

Beach

Jampore Beach

Nov 12

Bored of the same old beaches of Goa? Then plan a trip to the charming city of Daman which boasts of virgin palm-lined beaches. Jampore Beach is one such pristine beach in the city which is one of the best places to visit in Daman, known for numerous adventure activities, enthralling views of the expansive Arabian Sea, and a serene environment to give you a break from the bustling life of the city. Location: Jampore Beach Road, Daman Things To Do At Jampore Beach: Parasailing. car racing, camel/ horse ride, beach sports, and relaxing in the shacks Time Required: 1-2 hours How To Reach: Take a cab from Naini Damin taxi stand What’s Special: Walk Entry Fee: Free

Great Place For Kids

Devka Amusement Park

Nov 11

One of the best Daman tourist places is the Devka Amusement Park. A simple, old and basic park located by the Devka beach offers various joyrides, boasts a large garden, a food court with special ice cream parlour. It is one of the most amazing things to do in Daman if you are on a family vacation and especially visiting with kids. Get the amazing opportunity to remember your childhood as you can also enjoy camel and pony rides as well. Location: Devka Beach Rd, Opp.Hotel Ashoka Palace, Nani Daman, Daman, Daman and Diu 396210 Timing: 9 am – 9 pm Time Required: 1 hour. How To Reach: Alongside Devka Beach road, cab, or local transport available. What’s Special: Small rides, beaches. Entry Fee: INR 5.
